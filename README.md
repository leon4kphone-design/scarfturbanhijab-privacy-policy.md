# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** July 2026

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how our Pinterest Analytics application ("Application") collects, uses, and protects your information. We are committed to maintaining your privacy and ensuring transparency in our data practices.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Pinterest Data
When you connect your Pinterest account, we collect:
- Pinterest account information (username, profile ID)
- Pin performance metrics (impressions, clicks, saves, engagement)
- Board information and pin data
- Analytics data for tracking campaign performance

### 2.2 Usage Information
We may collect:
- Application usage patterns and features accessed
- Technical information (browser type, IP address, device type)
- Error logs and crash reports for troubleshooting

### 2.3 Authentication Data
- OAuth tokens and API credentials (stored securely)
- Account connection status

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to:
- Provide Pinterest analytics and insights
- Generate performance reports and metrics
- Improve and optimize the Application
- Diagnose technical issues
- Comply with legal obligations

**We do NOT:**
- Sell your data to third parties
- Share personal information without consent
- Use data for marketing purposes beyond improving the service
- Store passwords or sensitive credentials insecurely

## 4. Data Storage and Security

- All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS)
- API tokens are stored securely with industry-standard encryption
- We implement access controls and authentication measures
- Data is retained only as long as necessary for service operation

## 5. Third-Party Services

This Application integrates with:
- **Pinterest API** - Subject to Pinterest's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy (https://policy.pinterest.com/privacy-policy)
- Standard infrastructure providers for hosting and analytics

We do not share your data with additional third parties without your consent.

## 6. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Request access to your personal data
- Request deletion of your data
- Disconnect your Pinterest account at any time
- Request information about how your data is used

To exercise these rights, contact us via this GitHub repository.

## 7. Data Retention

- Pinterest analytics data is retained as long as you use the Application
- Upon account disconnection, we delete associated authentication tokens
- Historical data may be retained for up to 30 days

## 8. Compliance

This Application complies with:
- GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) for EU users
- CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)
- Pinterest's Developer Agreement and Policy
- Applicable data protection laws

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted here with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the Application indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
